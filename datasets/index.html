Sync protocol

Canonical authority is alexanarch.org. Mirror copies at this observatory exist to permit reading when the canonical is unavailable, and to preserve state at the moment of mirroring. Mirrors are refreshed by executing the following from the observatory's repo root:

$ ./datasets/sync-from-alexanarch.sh
  ↓ pulls each mirrored dataset from alexanarch.org
  ↓ writes to /datasets/<name>/
  ↓ verifies sha256 against MANIFEST.json where present
  ✓ canonical always wins on conflict
